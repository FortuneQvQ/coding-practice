四川大学生命科学学院诚聘海内外英才
发布时间：2024-03-01
四川大学位于四川省成都市锦江河畔，始建于1896年，是中国西部建立最早的现代高等学府，为国家“985”高校和双一流建设高校。四川大学生命科学学院历史悠久，始建于1916年。现有生物学和生态学2个一级学科博士授权点 及植物学、遗传学两个国家重点学科。 本科专业设有生物科学（含生物科学基地、生物技术基地和生物科学拔尖班）、生态学和交叉办学计算生物学专业。2017年教育部学科评估，生物学科被评为为A - 类，生态学科为B + 类，“资源生物学与高原生态学”列入双一流大学重点建设学科。学院拥有国家级生物科学实验教学示范中心和国家级生物科学与技术虚拟仿真实验教学中心，所属四川大学自然博物馆馆藏有植物标本80万份，动物标本14万份,馆藏标本居全国高校前茅。学院现有教职工168人，其中：国家杰出青年、优青计划、国家级教学名师、百千万人才等国家级人才达到28人次，高级职称有124人，其中博士生导师60人。 学院现拥有 生物资源与生态环境教育部重点实验室、四川省濒危野生动物保护生物学部省共建实验室、能源植物生物燃油制备及利用国家地方联合工程实验室和6个省级重点实验室。学院所属专业学科中的生物学与生物化学、分子生物学与遗传学、植物学与动物学、农业科学、生态学与环境科学进入ESI世界前0.5%。学院特诚邀海内外英才到本学院工作，一起共谋发展，开创四川大学生命科学学院的辉煌未来。 四川大学生命科学学院现面向海内外长期招聘高层次人才： 一、招聘领域和学科 1、招聘：植物学、动物学、动物医学、农业科学、生态学、遗传学、生物信息学、微生物学、细胞生物学、生物化学与分子生物学、生物医学等与生物学相关的优秀人才。 2、符合动植物物种基因资源及其前沿共性技术；濒危和特色物种保护与开发利用；植物生物灾害控制；动物健康与产品安全；高原生态保护和可持续发展等方向的优秀人才优先。 二、招聘对象 1、符合专家计划全职项目申请条件的人才; 2、国内外著名高校或科研机构的杰出和优秀人才; 3、在国内外著名高校获得博士学位、年龄在35岁以下的优秀青年学者。 三、工作及生活待遇 各类招聘人才实行PI制和薪酬年薪制 1、对符合条件的人才，年薪不低于40-100万元人民币， 薪酬根据入选者具体情况面议。 2、住房、配偶工作及子女入托、入学等生活待遇从优。 3、科研启动经费、实验室面积等根据入选者具体情况面议。 四、应聘程序与相关材料 有意者请将个人简历请发送至邮箱lifehrscu@163.com，有意申报国家优秀青年基金的人员，学院全力协助。 联系地址：四川省成都市武侯区望江路29号四川大学生命科学学院，610064 联系人：谢老师 电话：86-28-85412053 E-mail:lifehrscu@163.com 学院主页： http://life.scu.edu.cn/ College of Life Sciences, Sichuan University Calls for Global Talents Located in Chengdu, the capital city of Sichuan Province, China, Sichuan University (SCU) is the oldest (from 1896) modern university in West China. It is also one of the State 985 Project and Double First Class universities. Established in 1916, College of Life Sciences is one of the oldest colleges in SCU. Nowadays, there are two first-level disciplines authorized to offer doctorate degree in the college:Biology and Ecology, as well as two national key disciplines: Botany and Genetics. For undergraduate study, the college presently has threemajors: Biological Science（including Base of Biological Science, Base of Biotechnology and Biological Top Class）, Ecology and Computational Biology. In 2017, Biological Science in Sichuan University has been recognized as Class A-, and Ecology has been evaluated as B+ in the disciplinary evaluation of the Ministry of Education. Inaddition, our Bio-resources and Plateau Ecology research has been included in China’s construction plan of world-class universities and first-class disciplines (also known as “Double-First Class”). We also have two training bases for National Biological Talents and National Biological Science & Technology Talents. As well as the Basic Academic Talents Experimental Class from Ministry of education, the National Bio Science Experimental Teaching Demonstration Center and the Virtual Simulation Experimental Teaching Center of National Biological Science and Technology. Our Natural Museum, which already has 90 years’ history, is one of the biggest university museums in China. Nowadays it has already collected more than 800,000 plant specimens and 140,000 animal specimens.Nowadays, there are about 168 faculty members in the college，including 19 distinguished Professors(full time NationalDistinguishedExperts, Changjiang Scholar by Ministry of Education, National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars，as well as recipients of NationalYoung Program, National Teaching Award, National Program). In total, there are 124 professors (associate professors or above) and 60 PhD supervisors. Currently there are several excellent research centers in the college, such as Key Laboratory of Bio-resource and Ecology, Ministry of Education, National and Local Joint Engineering Laboratory for Energy Plant Bio-Oil Production and Application, as well as six provincial key laboratories. Recently, our Biology，Biochemistry，Molecular Biology and Genetics research , Botany and Animal Science, Agronomy, as well as ecology/environmental science rank top 0.5% in ESI. In the process of “Double-First Class” construction plan, we are specifically interested in Bio-resources and Plateau Ecology research. We are aiming to contribute for the building of innovative country, by advancing the fundamental study and application of specialized ecology system and bio-resource. Our college welcome talents from home and abroad, inaugurating bright future forthecollege together.
